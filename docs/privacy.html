Privacy Policy / 개인정보처리방침

Last updated: 2025-09-29

English

Camell Drive ("the App") is provided by GLOTN ("we"). We do not collect, sell, or share personal data for tracking purposes.

  • Camera/Microphone/Photos: Used only to capture or select media at your request. Media may be stored locally or uploaded to your chosen storage/server to provide the App's core functionality.
  • Files/Documents: Accessed only through the system picker for items you explicitly select.
  • Media Library: Used to read/save media you choose to import or export.
  • Network: Required to connect to your configured server endpoints for uploads/downloads.
  • Diagnostics: We may collect anonymized crash logs or performance data solely to improve stability. These logs do not identify you.
  • Account/Login: If sign-in is required, credentials are transmitted over secure connections for authentication only. Not used for advertising or tracking.
  • Third-Party Services: The App may connect to your own server endpoints. Your use of those services is governed by their policies.

Your Choices: You can revoke camera/microphone/photos permissions in iOS Settings at any time. File access occurs only when you initiate the system picker.

Contact: support@glotn.co.kr
